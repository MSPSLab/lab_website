Marine Self-Powered System Lab led by Prof. Minyi Xu, is committed to ocean blue energy and explores new ways of self-powered systems to reduce carbon footprint and be more energy independent. We are part of the Department of Marine Engineering at the Dalian Maritime University.

Our research areas include ocean energy, underwater sensing, maritime rescue, ship inspection, smart nacelles, green and healthy ship technology. Our desire is to achieve partial energy independence through self-powered system and to create more dynamic and exiting research in energy harvesting, sensors, communication, and control application. We embrace the values of integrity, excellence and teamwork, and we practice integrity in all its forms and are honest and fair to other team members and collaborators; We have a zero tolerance policy for plagiarism and falsification of results; We strive for excellence in everything we do and insist on scientific quality; Our goal is to develop hands-on individuals with excellent analytical, technical and communication skills; Ideas are freely shared and discussed, and we believe that great teamwork is the key to great science.; And we strive to build great collaborative relationships within and outside the group. With a truly diverse culture, each member of our team is passionate and committed to the research area of interest, we want to work with like-minded partners and encourage applicants from diverse backgrounds to join our team.
